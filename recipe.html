Blueberry-Lemon Chicken Breakfast Pops

By Ramiro Trejo

A great reciple to make during the summer mornings right before going to work or an important meeting. You might want to share them with your boss and coworkers!

Ingredients

      1 cup blueberries
      1/2 cup low-fat plain yogurt
      3 tablespoons pure maple syrup
      Zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
      Kosher salt
      Chiken meat

Directions

  1. Combine the blueberries, yogurt, maple syrup, lemon zest and juice and a pinch of salt in a blender and puree until smooth.
  2. Add the chicken meat to the mixture.
  3. Divide the mixture evenly among four 3- to 4-ounce ice pop molds and freeze until solid, at least 8 hours and preferably overnight. Let sit at room temperature for a few minutes before unmolding.
  4. The pops will keep in the freezer for up to 2 weeks or longer until somone has the guts to try them.
